# public_vault


## Obsidian: my tool of choice


This repository was created *for* and *with* **Obsidian**. This app allows us to create our own little second minds. I think that "second mind" is something we'll only be hearing more and more about as time goes on, as I truly believe that documenting and keeping track of information is something that adds incredible value to every effort in our lives; indeed, humans have been writing things down for centuries. I am not going to claim that our written tradition is more valuable than our equally as rich oral traditions, but what I will say is that we are currently living in a world of files. In this fastmoving world, I would say that it has never been more important to be able to slow down, take one step back, and create a space where we can see clearly all of the things happening in our lives. 

Obsidian allows us to create a network of thoughts, so everyone of us can be philosopher. It allows us to create lists and maps of all of the things that are important to us--all of the things we interreact with on a daily basis--so we never have to wonder where we put that file or note or thought. It allows us to keep track of everything from grocery lists to life goals (and even how these things connect to each other). I don't think I'll ever get sick of Obsidian, and I hope that you, dear reader, will find as much joy in it as I do! 

Anyways, these are all things that dozens (maybe hundreds) of people on the internet have already said, so I'm not going to bore you with anymore yapping about how important it is that we have our own thinking spaces. 


## About my Obsidian vault 

### How to use the vault

I uploaded this vault to my github (`haugsjaalean-commits`) with the `.obsidian` folder. This means that, if you so choose, you may download all of the settings, themes, and plugins that I use. My vault is configured as an arborescence starting at the `O.md` file. I use the [[Hotkeys]] plugin to make accessing it very easy. If you do not choose to copy my `.obsidian` folder, and you only want my notes, then consider reading [[Starting with Obsidian]] to better understand all of the things I use to make my vaults. 

### What this vault means to me


This vault is a place for me to share my thoughts, ideas, and even some learning material. I think that ideas are not something to be bought, sold, and owned, but shared openly with all who care, so I plan on making the effort to share as many of my ideas as possible! 

Ideas have always been very special and important to me, so, if I leave nothing of value on this Earth when my time comes to leave it, I will have at least given back my own thoughts inspired by the things I have had the privilege to experience here. That is what I hope to do with this vault: share my tinny window to this world we all live on. I think that it's by looking through as many windows as possible that we can become closer and kinder as a people. 




### This vault going forwards


I plan on continuing to improve and update all of the thoughts I choose to share here.
