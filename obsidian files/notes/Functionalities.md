## A note on the capabilities of Obsidian


Obsidian has lots of little things to discover in terms of what it's capable of doing, but I'm going to let you discover these for yourself and stick to the more complexe subjects in this tutorial. 

Some of the smaller functionalities that I'm referring would include include right clicking on just about anything to receive a great big list of options, or the use of `Ctrl + P`, which opens the command menue (which I honestly rarely use anyways). All of these things are most definitely explained in [Obsidian Help](https://obsidian.md/help/), so feel free to check that out.
