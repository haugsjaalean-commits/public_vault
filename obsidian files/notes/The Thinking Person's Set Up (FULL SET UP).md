## Notes on the tutorial
I watched this on 2x speed, because it's a lot of yapping. If 2x is too fast, feel free to slow it down, or feel free to skip the slow, boring, useless parts.

I didn't find this video very useful, but it was interesting to see her unique way of organizing notes, and I'm always interested to see the different ways people use Obsidian. ==This video **IS NOT** a must-watch==. I found Vicky Zhao's methods for organization to be a little bit rudimentary, although it could be argued that that's the strength of her system. She leans heavily on the use of tags, which I am typically against in most scenarios. In the end, she uses Obsidian for something very different from me, so our methods will not be the same. It's important to always know what *you* want from Obsidian, and this video is a great example of that. 

I take a far more structured approach to organization than her.
