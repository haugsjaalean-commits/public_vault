## Notes on the tutorial
This is a long one. I watched the whole thing, but you don't have to. Honestly he goes far and beyond what I do with bases (and Nick Milo overcomplicates things as usual). 

Even if he goes beyond what I do with bases, this video explains in an excellent manner many of the basics. On top of that, it also has a lot of rich ideas for organizing notes, so I would recommend watching if you have the time. Of course, you always have the option to watch on 2x speed!
