### 3 - The structure of the vault 


My vault has the structure of and arborescence. The whole arborescence starts in the root note which I have called "O" (but you can call it whatever you want). Using the [[Homepage]] plugin, we can easily come back to the root note (O) whenever we want. 

To simplify my whole theory, which I explain in [[Organization]], all we want to do is create pathways of links to lead us to the notes we want. On the internet, this type of pathway structure is often called a "MOC" ("Map Of Content"). We can create as many pathways as we want, and multiple pathways can lead to the same note because we're not in a folder structure, thank God!
