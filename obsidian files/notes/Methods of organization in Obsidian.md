# Mapping [[The theory of information|my theory]] onto Obsidian 


## What is "OOF"?

I think that, to achieve the best results and avoid wasting time thinking, it is best to always have a guideline which describes how to form *organization*. I am going to number all of these guidelines for simplicity's sake. I am also going to call the guidelines "OOF", which stands for "==Obsidian Organization Philosophy==".
