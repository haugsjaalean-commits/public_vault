

To get LYT Mode working, go to [this link](https://github.com/nickmilo/LYT-Mode) and download the files. Then, in the `.obsidian` folder in your vault, navigate to the *themes* folder. Here, you will add the `theme.css` and `manifest.json` files into a folder titled exactly *LYT Mode*. Enjoy! 

N.B. Sometimes, when multiple windows of Obsidian are open, the theme seems to have visual bugs, so be mindful of that.
