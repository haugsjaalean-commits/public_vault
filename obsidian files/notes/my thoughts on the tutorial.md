


I think the tutorial has a nice structure, but it could definitely be improved. Beyond the fact that my wording is often sloppy and heavy, I have an idea for completely restructuring the tutorial for more clarity. I have now realized that it would be nice to have a distinction between *tutorial* and *how I do it*. What I mean by this is that I currently have put my explanation of how to use Obsidian right alongside my personal preferences when it comes to using it. I actually think that this is a failure point of many tutorials out there on YouTube: they are rendered slow and heavy by the fact that personal use and *how to* are mixed together. It's slightly frustrating that I did not think of this sooner, but I think that most of my work can be recycled; however, I will not be making the change right away .
